Company Overview

Africa's Trusted Name in Animal Health Manufacturing

Haybee Agrosciences Enterprises has been at the forefront of veterinary pharmaceutical manufacturing in Nigeria for over three decades. From our facility across our GMP production facility (28 Ibaro Road, Ijagemo, Lagos) and head office (278 Ijegun Road, Ikotun, Lagos), we manufacture a comprehensive range of animal health solutions trusted by poultry farmers, aquaculture producers, livestock operators, and wholesale distributors nationwide.

Our deep market penetration, established distribution channels, and commitment to product quality have made Haybee a household name in Nigerian animal agriculture — and a growing presence in regional African markets.

Our Story

Three Decades of Commitment to Animal Health in Africa

Haybee Agrosciences Enterprises was founded with a singular purpose: to provide Nigerian farmers with access to high-quality, affordable veterinary pharmaceutical products manufactured on Nigerian soil.

Operating from our GMP production facility in Ijagemo and head office in Ikotun, Lagos, we have grown from a local veterinary supplier into one of Nigeria's recognized animal health manufacturers — serving thousands of veterinary professionals, farmers, and distributors nationwide.

Mission

To manufacture trusted veterinary pharmaceuticals that improve animal health, productivity and food security across Nigeria and Africa.

Vision

To be Africa's most trusted veterinary pharmaceutical manufacturer — expanding from Nigeria to serve the entire continent.

Integrity

We manufacture products we would trust with our own animals. No compromises on quality, efficacy or safety — ever.

Innovation

Continuously developing formulations tailored to specific disease challenges facing African livestock and aquaculture farmers.

Company Milestones

Our Journey

Early 1990s
Haybee Agrosciences Enterprises founded in Lagos, Nigeria. Initial focus on veterinary pharmaceutical distribution and supply to local farms.
Late 1990s
Expansion into pharmaceutical manufacturing. First Haybee-branded veterinary products introduced. NAFDAC product registration begins.
2000s
Major portfolio expansion. Antibiotics, anticoccidials and nutritional supplements introduced. Nationwide distribution network established.
2010s
Entry into aquaculture. Fishmycin launched as Nigeria's fish farming industry booms. First export activities initiated.
2020s — Present
Full portfolio modernization. Digital transformation. Regional export expansion. Investor readiness and preparation for African market leadership.
